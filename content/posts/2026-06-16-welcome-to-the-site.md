This is the first post on my new site.

For a long time my writing, my course handouts, and the teaching materials  lived in scattered folders, email attachments, and learning-management
systems that students lose access to the moment a term ends. I wanted one stable,
public place that I control.

So this site has three jobs:

1. **Writing**: essays and shorter notes from anything to everything.
2. **Teaching**: handouts and readings my current students can download any time, with no login.
3. **Resources**: teaching packs and templates colleagues are welcome to adapt.
